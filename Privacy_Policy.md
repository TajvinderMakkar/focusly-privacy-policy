# Privacy Policy for Focusly - Task Manager

**Last Updated:** June 12, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Focusly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Focusly mobile application.

By using Focusly, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you create an account or use Focusly, we may collect:
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Name**: To personalize your experience
- **Profile Information**: Display name and avatar (optional)

### 1.2 Task and Productivity Data
- **Tasks**: Titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities, completion status
- **Daily Tasks**: Recurring tasks and completion streaks
- **Weekly Planner**: Scheduled activities and time blocks
- **Calendar Events**: Date-based task organization
- **Analytics Data**: Task completion rates, productivity metrics (stored locally and in cloud)

### 1.3 Device Information
- **Device Type**: Android version, device model
- **App Usage Data**: Features accessed, crash reports, performance metrics
- **Notifications**: Push notification tokens (for reminders and alerts)

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Data
- **Log Data**: IP address, device identifiers, timestamps
- **Analytics**: App usage patterns, feature interactions (via Firebase Analytics)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Core Features**: Task management, reminders, scheduling, analytics
- **Account Management**: Authentication via Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In
- **Cloud Sync**: Securely backup and sync your tasks across devices
- **Send Notifications**: Remind you about upcoming tasks and deadlines
- **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries and troubleshoot issues
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- Tasks and settings are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- Secure storage using Android Keystore for sensitive data

### 3.2 Cloud Storage
- Your data is backed up to **MongoDB Atlas** (our secure cloud database)
- Encrypted connections via HTTPS/TLS
- Data is stored in secure, industry-standard data centers

### 3.3 Firebase Services
- **Firebase Authentication**: Secure login via email/password and Google Sign-In
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
- We follow Google's Firebase security best practices

### 3.4 Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure authentication tokens
- Regular security audits
- No third-party access to your personal data

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information.**

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Google Firebase**: Authentication and analytics

### 4.2 Legal Compliance
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before any transfer occurs.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Update
- You can view, edit, and delete your tasks anytime within the app
- Update your profile information in Settings

### 5.2 Data Export
- Export your tasks as JSON or CSV files (Backup & Restore feature)
- Download your data anytime via Settings → Backup

### 5.3 Data Deletion
- **Delete Individual Tasks**: Swipe left on any task
- **Clear All Data**: Settings → Clear All Data (permanent deletion)
- **Delete Account**: Contact us at tajvindermakkar@gmail.com to request full account deletion

### 5.4 Opt-Out Options
- **Notifications**: Disable in Settings → Notifications
- **Analytics**: Disable Firebase Analytics in app settings (coming soon)
